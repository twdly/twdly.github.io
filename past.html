Past

This page is about the history of my experiences with computing so far.

Early Days

My passion for computers, like many others I'd assume, began with playing games from a young age. I still remember watching dad play Crash Bandicoot 2 on our PS2 which was the newest console for the time. The first console that I ever owned myself was a DS Lite which I used to put an extensive amount of time into various games across numerous franchises with the most notable ones being Mario and Pokemon.

Growing Interests

Sometime during 2015 or 2016, I stumbled across a video of someone building a PC. Although I can no longer find the video, I distinctly remember it involving the person building the PC spray painting all the components green. It was this video that made me want to build my own computer. Several years later, at the end of 2019, I had successfully researched and procured the parts to my first PC build with the specs as follows:
